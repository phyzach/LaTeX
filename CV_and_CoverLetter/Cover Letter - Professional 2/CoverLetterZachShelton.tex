Date: \today \par \vspace{-0.1cm}

To whom it may concern,

At Norden, global trade and the networks that make them up require constant analysis and oversight in order to minimize costs and delivery times. At a glance this is system feels chaotic and indescernible, similar to the quantum world of particle physics. I see complicated systems as a challenge to overcome and describe, my academic background and my recent work as a data engineer put me in an opportunity to contribute to NORDEN's Data Engineering Team and create impact(no matter how small) at a global scale in a positive way.

During my studies, I collaborated with a multinational team on the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) Experiment at CERN, where I developed machine learning algorithms to analyze large-scale datasets. My work involved creating predictive models to isolate specific decay events from kinematics data using neural networks, decision trees, and ML libraries such as XGBoost, TensorFlow, and Keras. These tools used were new to me at the time, I am more than capable of quickly getting up to speed regardless of the tool or environment.

At Wolfram Research, I taught Wolfram Language and guided university students on data-intensive projects, including financial modeling, statistical analysis, and machine learning applications. These projects spanned diverse fields such as GIS, mathematics, physics, and chemistry, giving me the adaptability to quickly learn new domains and communicate insights effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. I treated the students' project in a way similar to a SCRUM attempting to maintain proper timelines and accountability. These experiences put me in the perfect position to quickly join existing structues and teams to quickly contribute to existing and new databases and analysis projects.


Most recently, I worked as a Data Engineer at Uptimus, a Danish startup focused on last-mile delivery optimization for clients like Bring and Nemlig. I designed future data-streaming and integration solutions for an ETL pipeline, enabling the mapping of data from various vehicle makes and models to Uptimus' internal tracking system. My work involved preparing production-ready scripts using tools like Docker, Azure, PostgreSQL, GitHub, and QGIS. This role strengthened my proficiency in core Python libraries (such as pandas, NumPy, GeoPandas and SciPy). At Norden, I can enhance current data pipelines and provide insight to business partners and my colleagues.

At NORDEN, I hope to join a well-organized development environment and grow my technical skills alongside colleagues at a company focused on making a positive impact in Denmark and in the world. I can provide references including my supervisor at Uptimus, who can speak to my technical abilities and work ethic. Please feel free to contact me via phone or email to discuss my application or arrange an interview.
